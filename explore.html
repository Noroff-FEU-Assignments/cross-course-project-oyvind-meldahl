Our Jackets

All of our jackets can be worn by everyone, of course! The only difference between jackets for men and women is some subtle differences in sizing around the waist and hip, where the female models usually is wider at the hip and smaller at the waist than the male models. There is no colour-choice, what you see on-site is what is available at the moment.

You still get the same excellent quality, same 5 year-guarantee and the right to return the jacket to us if you are not completely satisfied. All our jackets undergo rigorous quality-controls, and all of the different models are stress-tested outside in real weather, real terrain, real adventure!
