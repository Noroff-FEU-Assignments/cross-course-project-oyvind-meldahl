About us

We are a small but proud company making jackets we would like to wear ourselves.

all of us are going to climb Mt. Everest. Maybe your adventure is the evening walk with the family dog. In the rain. Ahh, nothing quite as fun as walking a wet dog in pouring rain. But at least you will be dry and warm if you are wearing a Rainydays jacket. Maybe you'll even get some enjoyment of it? Not being bothered by the rain, maybe the adventure around the neighbourhood will be enjoyable.

For it is an adventure. After all, it seems you are the only one that was brave enough to step outside tonight. Everybody else is inside. Make it your adventure. Push your comfort zone.

The idea for the company was born in 2018, and the first jacket went for sale in 2019. We are a small group of friends that love to be outside in all kinds of weather, and we know first hand what we want a jacket to be.

We want you to love the outdoors just as much as we do. And to do that you need to be warm and dry. Use our jackets, push your comfort zone.
